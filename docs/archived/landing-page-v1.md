## How it works

Enter your address. That's it.

Lawn Agent pulls your climate, your soil, your grass type, and your season. It tells you what your lawn needs right now — and what to do about it. No setup wizard. No questionnaire. No expertise required.

When your pre-emergent window opens, you'll know. When it's time to overseed, you'll know. When something's off, you'll know.

## V1 scope note

This is v1 copy, written to accurately reflect the product at launch. Two lines in particular are intentionally conservative and should be revisited for v2:

**1. The commerce line:**

> "shows you exactly what to buy and gets it in your cart — one tap"

This reflects the v1 deep link mechanic — app surfaces the right product and quantity, opens a pre-filled Amazon or Home Depot cart, user completes checkout. When full commerce integration ships — where Lawn Agent completes the order on the user's behalf — this line should be upgraded to reflect the stronger promise. Something closer to the original "gets the right products to your door" becomes honest at that stage.

**2. The approval/ordering flow:**
The current copy implies a one-tap cart experience. When the full proposal/approval/order pipeline is live, the copy should reflect the complete agentic loop — propose, approve, done. That's a meaningfully stronger story and deserves its own headline treatment in v2.

V2 copy should be written after commerce integration is scoped and confirmed — not before.
